Preparing for a Hurricane

  1. Plan evacuation route
  2. Purchase non-perishable food, batteries, matches, drinking water, flashlights, and first aid supplies
  3. Review insurance policies
  4. Take inventory of all valuables
  5. Cut weak branches and trees near your house
  6. Seal outer wall openings

Source: https://www.iii.org/article/preparing-hurricane

During a Hurricane

  1. Listen to the radio or TV for real-time information
  2. Secure your home, close storm shutters, and secure outdoor objects or bring them indoors
  3. Turn off utilities if recommended. Otherwise, turn the refrigerator thermostat to its coldest setting and keep its doors closed.
  4. Turn off propane tanks.
  5. Ensure a supply of water for sanitary purposes such as cleaning and flushing toilets.
  6. Fill the bathtub and other large containers with water.

Source: https://emergency.yale.edu/be-prepared/hurricane

Hurricane Recovery

  1. Avoid contact with flood water
  2. Boil water for one minute before drinking
  3. Remove standing water
  4. Leave mold control and other serious damage to professionals

https://www.epa.gov/natural-disasters/hurricanes#recover
